## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does AI food recognition work?
Take a photo of your meal and our AI will identify the food items and estimate calorie and macro content automatically.

### How does barcode scanning work?
Point your camera at any food barcode (EAN-13 or UPC). Kalorie pulls nutrition data from a comprehensive food database.

### What's included in the free tier?
- Manual food logging (unlimited)
- Barcode scanning (unlimited)
- 3 AI food scans 
- 3 AI nutritionist messages 
- 7-day analytics view

### What does Premium include?
- Unlimited AI food scans and nutritionist chats
- 30-day and 90-day analytics
- Messaging and social features
- Achievements and streaks
- Apple HealthKit integration
- $9.99/month

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings > Manage Subscription, or manage it directly through your Apple ID settings.

### How is my data protected?
Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your personal information. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md) for details.
